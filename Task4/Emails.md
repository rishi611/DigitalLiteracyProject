## Project Overview

So this is basically my Digital Literacy Portfolio, which I put together as part of the CSE0001 course assignment. Made a one-page infographic on Canva. It basically covers what digital literacy means, safe internet habits, and how students should present themselves professionally online. 📁 [View files](https://claude.ai/chat/task-1-presentation/)
